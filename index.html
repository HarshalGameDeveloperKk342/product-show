Cleave 300 Side Table

Cleave is a series of sleek tables where the metal legs intersect with the solid stone block and branch into two parts at same or different levels forming the table top. The stone block acts as an anchor, while the riveted metal sheets fasten to it.

Available Size (in mm)

500 W x 300 D x 500 H

Details

Top and Structure - In bent sheet metal
Base - In stone

Designed By

Wriver Design Studio

Finishes

Metal

  • Brass Antique Wrinkled
  • Black Nickel Antique Wrinkled
  • Copper Antique Wrinkled

Stone/Tiles

  • Zura Travertine (Honed)
  • White Carrara (Polished)
  • Ebony Travertine (Honed)
  • Black Marquina (Polished)
